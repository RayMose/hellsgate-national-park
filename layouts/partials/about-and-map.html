Discover Hell's Gate

Nestled in the heart of Kenya's Great Rift Valley, Hell's Gate National Park is a testament to the raw beauty of nature.

Named for its intense geothermal activity, this park offers a unique blend of dramatic landscapes, diverse wildlife, and thrilling adventures. From towering cliffs and deep gorges to open savannah and bubbling hot springs, Hell's Gate is a microcosm of Kenya's natural wonders.

  • Spectacular rock formations including Fischer's Tower and Central Tower
  • Home to over 100 species of birds, including the rare Lammergeier vulture
  • One of the few national parks where walking, cycling, and rock climbing are permitted

Experience the thrill of walking among wildlife, cycle through breathtaking gorges, or test your climbing skills on ancient volcanic plugs. Hell's Gate National Park offers an adventure like no other in Kenya.
