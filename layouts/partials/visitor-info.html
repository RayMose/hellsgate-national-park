Visitor Information

Park Hours

Open daily from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Entrance Fees

  • Adults: $25
  • Children (3-18): $15
  • Kenyan Residents: 300 KSH

Getting Here

Located 100 km northwest of Nairobi. Accessible by car or organized tour.

Accommodation

Camping sites available. Nearby lodges and hotels in Naivasha town.

Best Time to Visit

Year-round, but dry seasons (Jan-Mar and Jul-Oct) are most comfortable.

Safety Tips

  • Stay on designated trails
  • Carry plenty of water
  • Respect wildlife - maintain distance
